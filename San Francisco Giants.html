About the team

A brief history of the organization

The San Francisco Giants are part of the National League West Division along with the Los Angeles Dodgers, San Diego Padres, Arizona Diamondbacks, and the Colorado Rockies. The team was founded in 1883 and has won eight World Series championships, having the most franchise success in the early 2010's, with World Series Titles in 2010, 2012, and 2014. The Giants have played their home games at Oracle Park since 2000. See the San Francisco Giants Wikipedia Page.

World Series

The Giants have won eight World Series Championships. These include 1905,1921,1922,1933,1954, 2010,2012, and 2014

Bruce Bochy

In his 13 years as manager for the Giants, Bochy earned the team three World Series Championships in the span of five years.
